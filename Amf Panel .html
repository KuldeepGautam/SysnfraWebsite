AMF Panel (Transfer Switch)

SISPL Auto mains failure system (AMF) is one of the flagship products of system infra solutions, it can work seamlessly to work seamlessly either on 1ø/2ø/3ø supply, and it enhances power utilization from DG, Mains, Battery and Solar (Optional), it has an additional feature of remote management.

AMF follows a rational approach towards power management through

  1. AC Power measurement (1 ø/2 ø/3 ø)
  2. DG Parameter Monitoring & control
  3. Alarm consolidation
  4. GPRS Remote Management (optional)
  5. Fuel Management (Optional)

Power Rating supported by AMF panel is 15 KVA to 30 KVA.

The state of art controller is quite intelligent and comes with remote monitoring and controlling over a wireless network, which is an optional feature.
