System Infra Solutions Private Limited (SISPL) India’s largest telecom products Manufacturer as well as Software Development, Mobile App Development & customized hardware solutions.

SISPL having a manufacturing facility of 7000 Square feet for Telecom products, also has a very strong team to provide services & solutions impacting the communications world. Our culture of continuous innovation in our products and solutions has been instrumental in generating value of our customers.

SISPL manufactures Telecom Equipment like Automation Controller, AMF Panel, DC Energy Meter, Dual DG (Automation)Panel, I-protect, RMS(Remote Monitoring System), Universal AC Controller, DC to DC Converter, Energy Controller, Low Voltage Disconnect, Retrofit Controller, Home Automation, Dual DG Controller, Digital Voltage Corrector, Signange Controller, DG Relay Card, I2 PMS Complete Solution, NanoMac, Solar Energy Management System, Static Voltage Regulator Card (SVR Card), Free Cooling Unit and other telecom related similar products. With NOC Solution for telecom, SISPL is established in the telecom sector since October 2000 with a vision of becoming an end-to-end cost-effective Infrastructure Support Partner (ISP) to the Telecom Industry.

Mobile App: SIS-AXS, WE-SIS, I-Protect, Customized mobile app solution
